GDPR Compliance

In alignment with the GDPR, SFA-API provides the following rights: the right to be informed, the right of access, the right to rectification, the right to erasure, the right to restrict processing, the right to data portability, the right to object and also rights around automated decision making and profiling. The full documentation for this API can be found in the SFA Github repository.
